Small Mammal Diversity & Evolution

How many species of mammals are on an island? Does the current taxonomy reflect their evolutionary history? These are the kind of questions I seek to answer in my research. For my dissertation, I am focusing on the Bunomys Clade, a group of mice endemic to Sulawesi island in Indonesia. I am also involved in other projects on Southeast Asian shrews.

Specimen Digitization

This project is led by Dr. Nurainas at the Herbarium Universitas Andalas (ANDA) funded through the collection and data mobilization grant provided by the Global Biodiversity Information Facility (GBIF). Project progress is available here.

Open Source Software

I utilize language agnostic approches to improve efficiency and reproducibility in conducting field-based evolutionary studies. I use Rust for developing performance critical software, such as for phylogenomic analyses. For GUI based projects, I use Flutter framework and the Dart programming language.
