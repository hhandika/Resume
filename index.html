Hi,

I am Heru Handika. I am a PhD candidate at Esselstyn's Lab, Louisiana State University, USA. My research interest centers around understanding how complex topography and geological history generate small mammal diversity. We conduct multi-nationality collaborative fieldwork on islands in Southeast Asia. Whenever relevant, I develop computer programs to improve efficiency in conducting our research projects.
