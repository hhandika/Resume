Hello, I am Heru.

I study mammals in Southeast Asia. My research asks these simple questions: what is a species? how do islands generate biodiversity? To answer the questions, I travel across islands and ascent across elevation on mountains in SE Asia. My work combines rigorous fieldwork to remote places and available scientific tools, so that we will have improved knowledge on species diversity. Currently, I am a PhD student in Esselstyn's Lab at the Museum of Natural Science and the Department of Biological Sciences, Louisiana State University, USA.

Research Interests

My main interests are biogeography, biodiversity, evolution
